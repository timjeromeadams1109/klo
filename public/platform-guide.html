CONFERENCE
⚙

Running a Conference

Admin guide to managing live events

  • Create sessions — Name each segment (e.g., "Morning Keynote", "Panel Discussion")
  • Toggle Seminar Mode on to activate conference tools for attendees
  • Choose a release mode: Show All, Single Release, or Hide All
  • Moderate questions — mark answered, hide, archive, or restore
  • Open the Monitor View on a projector for the audience display
Pro Tip: Create all sessions ahead of time. During the event, switch between them with one tap. See the Conference V2 Guide for detailed instructions.

FAQ — Getting Started

How do I sign up?
Visit keithlodom.io and click "Get Started." Sign in with your Google or Apple account — no separate password needed.
Can I try the platform for free?
Yes! The Explorer tier is completely free. You get two assessments, community feed access, and conference participation.
How do I upgrade my subscription?
Go to your profile or the pricing page and select Pro or Executive. Payment is processed securely through Stripe.
Which assessments are free?
Church Readiness and AI Readiness are free for all tiers. Governance and Cyber Risk require Pro or Executive.
Can I export my assessment results?
Yes — Pro and Executive tiers can export to PDF, Word, and PowerPoint. Explorer tier can view results on-screen only.

FAQ — Staff & Admin

How do I get admin access?
The platform Owner assigns Admin roles. Contact the Owner to request access — it takes effect immediately.
What's the difference between Admin and Moderator?
Admins manage the full dashboard (users, content, revenue). Moderators focus on live conference tools (Q&A, profanity, sessions).
How do I set up a conference?
Open the Conference tab in the admin dashboard, create sessions, then toggle Seminar Mode on when you're ready to go live.
How does the profanity filter work?
A customizable word list screens Q&A and word cloud submissions. Blocked attempts are logged in an audit trail for review.
Where do I view revenue data?
The Revenue tab in the admin dashboard shows MRR, tier breakdowns, subscription changes, and Stripe payment data.

FAQ
🚨

Troubleshooting

I can't sign in
Make sure you're using the same Google or Apple account you signed up with. Clear your browser cache and try again. If the issue persists, contact support.
My assessment won't load or save
Check your internet connection and refresh the page. If answers aren't saving, try a different browser. Your progress is auto-saved as you go.
AI Advisor says I've reached my limit
Pro tier has a 50-message monthly cap. Your count resets on your billing date. Upgrade to Executive for unlimited access.
Conference tools aren't showing
Conference tools only appear when Seminar Mode is active. Ask the event admin to confirm it's turned on. Try refreshing the page.
Export isn't working
Export requires Pro or Executive tier. If you're subscribed and still having issues, disable browser pop-up blockers and try again.
