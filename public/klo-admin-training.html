ADMIN SIDE

Accessing the Conference Admin

1
Sign in at klo-app.vercel.app/auth/signin using your admin credentials
2
Navigate to the Admin Dashboard (lock icon in navigation or go to /admin)
3
Click the "Conference" tab in the tab bar at the top
4
You'll see all management sections stacked vertically:
  • Seminar Mode Toggle
  • Session Management
  • Announcements
  • Poll Management
  • Question Moderation
  • Word Cloud
  • Profanity Filter
  • Role Management

Admin Roles

Owner (Keith)Full access to everything
AdminFull conference management
ModeratorQ&A moderation only

Seminar Mode

Toggle this ON before your event. It enables the live conference experience for guests. Toggle OFF when the event is over.

POLLING

Creating Polls

Method 1: Single Poll

1
In Poll Management, make sure "Single" mode is selected (toggle at top)
2
Type your poll question in the text field
3
Enter at least 2 answer options (click "+" to add more)
4
Click "Create Poll" — it goes to the Queued list

Key Point: Newly created polls are queued, not visible to guests yet. You must deploy them separately.

Method 2: Batch Input

Toggle to "Batch" mode and enter multiple polls using this format:

What is your role? | Executive | Manager | Staff
Rate the keynote | Excellent | Good | Fair | Poor
Best session today? | Session A | Session B | Session C

Each line = one poll. Separate question and options with | pipes.

Method 3: File Upload

Click the upload button and select a file:

.txt
.pdf
.docx
.xlsx

Same pipe-separated format. Up to 20 questions per upload.

POLLING

Deploying & Managing Polls

During Your Event

1
Pre-load your polls before the event (create them in Queued)
2
When ready, click "Deploy" on the poll you want to launch — it goes live instantly
3
Watch votes come in by clicking the expand chevron to see results in real-time
4
When done collecting votes, click the Power icon to close voting
5
Click the Eye icon to reveal results to the audience
6
Move on — deploy the next poll when ready

Pro Tips

  • Deploy one poll at a time to keep the audience focused
  • Give 60-90 seconds for voting before closing
  • Show results AFTER closing for maximum reveal impact
  • You can re-open a closed poll if needed (toggle power back on)

Important

Each guest can only vote once per poll. Votes are tracked by a unique device fingerprint — no account required. This prevents ballot stuffing while keeping participation frictionless.

Q&A

Moderating Q&A (Admin)

How It Works

When guests submit questions, they appear in the Question Moderation section of the Conference admin. Questions are unreleased by default — you decide which ones go live.

Question Controls

ActionIconEffect
ReleaseEyeMakes the question visible to all guests
Mark AnsweredCheck CircleAdds a green "Answered" badge
HideEye OffTemporarily hides from guest view
ArchiveArchiveMoves to the archived section (soft delete)
DeleteTrashPermanently removes (admin/owner only)

Question Sorting

Questions are automatically sorted by:

  • Likes (from authenticated users) — highest first
  • Upvotes (from anonymous guests) — then by these
  • Time submitted — newest last

This means the most popular questions naturally rise to the top.

Archived Questions

Archived questions move to a separate collapsible section at the bottom. You can restore them back to active at any time.

Profanity Filter

Questions are automatically checked for inappropriate language before being submitted. Blocked questions never reach you. Manage the word list in the Profanity Filter section.

Q&A

Recommended Q&A Workflow

1
Before the event: Make sure Seminar Mode is ON and the session has Q&A enabled in Session Management
2
As questions come in: Review them in the Question Moderation panel. They appear in real-time.
3
Release good questions by clicking the Eye icon — they become visible to all attendees
4
Let the audience upvote their favorite questions — the most popular rise to the top
5
Address the top questions during your Q&A segment, then mark them as Answered (check icon)
6
Archive or hide off-topic or duplicate questions to keep things clean

Live Q&A Strategy

  • Release questions in batches during designated Q&A breaks
  • Use upvote counts to prioritize which to answer first
  • The "Top 5" badge (gold star) highlights the most popular questions for guests
  • Marking as "Answered" shows a green badge so guests know it was addressed

Tip: Assign a moderator to manage Q&A while you focus on presenting. Add moderators in the Role Management section.

ANNOUNCEMENTS & WORD CLOUD

Announcements & Word Cloud

Push Announcements

Send real-time messages to every connected guest instantly.

1
Scroll to "Push Instructions / Announcements" in the Conference tab
2
Enter a Title (e.g., "Break Time") and a Message
3
Click "Push to Attendees"
4
A blue/cyan banner appears on every guest's screen instantly

Use announcements for: break times, room changes, WiFi info, schedule updates, or any real-time instructions.

Word Cloud

A fun, visual engagement tool. Guests submit words and they appear in a live word cloud.

Admin Controls

  • View all submitted words with frequency counts
  • See total unique words and total submissions
  • Top 50 most frequent words displayed
  • Clear All button resets the entire word cloud (with confirmation prompt)

Guest Experience

Guests type a word (max 30 characters) and submit. The word cloud updates live with:

  • Size based on frequency (more popular = bigger)
  • 7-color rotating palette
  • Random rotation for visual interest
GUEST SIDE

How Guests Join

1
Share the link klo-app.vercel.app/conference/join with your audience (on screen, in email, or via QR code)
2
Guests open the link on their phone, tablet, or laptop — no account or download required
3
They see the live conference page with tabbed sections:
Polls
Q&A
Word Cloud
4
Everything updates in real-time — when you deploy a poll or release a question, it appears instantly on their screen

Guest Requirements

  • Any modern web browser
  • Phone, tablet, or laptop
  • WiFi or cellular connection
  • No app download needed
  • No account or sign-up needed

Make Sure

Seminar Mode is ON before guests arrive. If it's off, the live conference features won't be active.

GUEST SIDE

Guest Experience: Voting on Polls

What the Guest Sees

1
When you deploy a poll, it appears under the "Polls" tab on every guest's screen
2
The poll question is shown with radio button options (one choice)
3
Guest selects their answer and taps "Submit Vote"
4
After voting, they see a confirmation. If you've enabled results, they see:
  • Horizontal progress bars per option
  • Percentage breakdown
  • Total vote count
  • Bar chart visualization

Guest Poll Rules

One vote per pollTracked by device fingerprint — cannot vote twice
No account neededVoting is completely anonymous
Real-timeNew polls appear instantly when deployed
ResultsOnly visible when admin enables "Show Results"
Closed pollsStill visible but "Submit" button is disabled

Audience Tip: Tell your audience "Take out your phones — there's a quick poll for you!" then deploy the poll. The immediate interaction gets people engaged.

GUEST SIDE

Guest Experience: Q&A

Submitting a Question

1
Guest taps the "Q&A" tab on the conference page
2
Types their question in the text input field
3
Optionally enters their name (or submits anonymously)
4
Taps "Submit" — the question goes to your moderation queue
5
If the profanity filter catches something, the guest sees an error with the flagged term(s)

What Happens Next

The question does not appear publicly until you (the admin) release it. This gives you full control over what's shown to the audience.

Browsing & Upvoting Questions

Once you release questions, all guests can see them and interact:

FeatureDetails
Upvote (chevron up)Anonymous, one per guest per question. Helps popular questions rise.
Like (heart icon)Only for signed-in users. Shows separately from upvotes.
"Answered" badgeGreen badge appears when you mark a question answered
"Top 5" badgeGold badge on the 5 most-liked questions

Real-Time Updates

All changes sync instantly via real-time subscriptions. When you release, answer, or hide a question, every guest sees the update within seconds.

REFERENCE

Quick Reference Card

Before the Event

  • Sign in and go to Admin → Conference tab
  • Turn Seminar Mode ON
  • Create and queue your polls (single, batch, or file upload)
  • Set up sessions if using session-specific Q&A
  • Review profanity filter word list
  • Assign moderators if needed
  • Test with the join link on your phone

During the Event

  • Deploy polls one at a time as needed
  • Close polls → reveal results for discussion
  • Monitor & release incoming questions
  • Mark questions as answered after addressing
  • Push announcements for logistics
  • Run word cloud for icebreakers

After the Event

  • Turn Seminar Mode OFF
  • Review any unanswered questions
  • Clear the word cloud if desired
  • Delete test polls if any remain
