3 Day-Of Setup (30 min before)

  1. Connect to venue WiFi on your laptop/tablet. Confirm klo-app.vercel.app loads.
  2. Log in as Owner at /auth/signin using Keith's credentials above.
  3. Open the Conference Monitor at /conference/monitor on your device (laptop or tablet). This is your real-time dashboard — keep it open throughout the session.
  4. Activate Seminar Mode. On the monitor page, toggle "Seminar Mode" ON. This unlocks the interactive tools (polls, Q&A, word cloud) for attendees. Tools stay locked until you activate this.
  5. Set up the projector. On the projected screen, open /conference/join in full-screen (press F11 or Cmd+Shift+F in Chrome). This shows the QR code for attendees to scan.
  6. Test with your phone. Scan the QR code, submit a test question, and verify it appears on the monitor. Delete the test question from the monitor afterward.
  7. Create your polls from the monitor page. You can create polls before the session starts and activate them at the right moment.

4 During the Session

Opening (first 2 minutes)

  • Show the QR code slide (/conference/join) on the projector
  • Say: "Scan this QR code to ask questions, vote in polls, and participate live. No login required — just point your camera."
  • Give attendees ~60 seconds to connect

Q&A

  • Attendees submit questions from their phones at /conference
  • Questions appear on your monitor in real-time with upvote counts
  • You can mark questions as "Answered" from the monitor
  • Questions with the most upvotes rise to the top

Live Polls

  • Create polls from the monitor page (you can pre-create them)
  • Activate a poll when you're ready — attendees see it instantly
  • Results update in real-time on both the monitor and audience view
  • Each person gets one vote per poll (works correctly on shared WiFi)

Word Cloud

  • Ask a question like "In one word, what does innovation mean to you?"
  • Attendees type their word on the conference page
  • The word cloud builds in real-time — popular words appear larger
✅ Optimized for 100+ users — shared WiFi voting, debounced real-time updates, server-side caching, and SQL-side aggregation ensure smooth performance even with a full room.

7 Troubleshooting

Attendees can't scan the QR code

  • Tell them to type klo-app.vercel.app/conference in their browser
  • Works on any phone — iPhone, Android, any browser

Polls/Q&A not showing for attendees

  • Make sure Seminar Mode is ON in the monitor page
  • If still not working, have attendees refresh their browser

Attendee says they already voted

  • Each browser session gets one vote per poll — this is by design
  • If they clear their browser or use incognito, they could vote again (acceptable edge case)

Page is slow or not updating

  • The app has CDN caching (2-5 seconds). Real-time updates use WebSockets — they should appear within 1 second
  • If an attendee's page seems stuck, a browser refresh fixes it

Need to remove an inappropriate question

  • From the monitor, click the question and delete/hide it
  • It disappears for all attendees immediately
